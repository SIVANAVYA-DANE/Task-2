Rohit Sharma fondly known as the "Hitman", is an Indian cricketer and a right-handed batsman who is known for his explosive batting style and elegant strokes. He is one of the most successful and prolific batsmen in the history of cricket.

Career Highlights

  • Born: April 30, 1987 in Bansod, Nagpur, Maharashtra, India
  • ODI Debut: June 23, 2007 against Ireland
  • Test Debut: November 6, 2013 against West Indies
  • Only player to have three double centuries in ODIs
  • Leading run-scorer in a single edition of the ICC World Cup (2019)
